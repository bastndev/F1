Welcome

What is My Skills?

My Skills is an editor extension that helps you create, install, and manage AI instructions for your projects. Think of it as a recipe book your coding assistant reads before working on your code.

Instead of repeating the same requests to your AI, you save them once as skills. Next time, your assistant already knows your style, rules, and workflow.

Create

Make your own skill — no code needed

Open the CREATE tab and follow the chat wizard. It is like texting a friend what you need.

  1. Pick a name for your skill (for example, my-react-rules).
  2. Choose a category: Web, Mobile, Backend, AI, and more.
  3. Describe what you want. The more details, the better the result. Aim for at least 60 characters or 10 words.
  4. Pick a template:
    • Base — a clean, simple skill in seconds.
    • Fast — detects your project technologies and writes a detailed guide.
    • AI — coming soon.
  5. Done! Your new skill appears in the LOCAL tab, ready to use.
💡

Tip: You can also create AGENTS.md, CLAUDE.md, or DESIGN.md with a single click from the CREATE screen.

Install

Find ready-made skills from the marketplace

The INSTALL tab is a store of community skills. Browse trending, official publishers, or search by keyword.

  1. Switch to the INSTALL tab.
  2. Browse All Time, Trending 🔥, or @ Official skills.
  3. Click the Install button on any skill you like.
  4. Choose where to install it:
    • .agents/skills/ — recommended for most projects.
    • .claude/skills/ — optimized for Claude Code.
  5. Your skill appears in the LOCAL tab, ready to activate.
💡

Tip: You can install skills directly from the CREATE search results too.

Local

Manage your workspace skills

The LOCAL tab shows everything installed in your current project. It is your control center.

  • Enable / Disable — toggle the switch next to any skill to turn it on or off.
  • Edit — open a skill file directly in your editor.
  • Delete — remove a skill you no longer need.
  • Save — store a skill in your personal library to reuse in other projects later.
  • Sort — reorder by newest, A–Z, or Z–A.
💡

Tip: Saved skills live in your Saved tab. Click Enable to bring one into your current workspace instantly.

FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to know how to code?

No. My Skills is designed for everyone. You describe what you want in plain English and the extension builds the files for you.

What is the difference between .agents/ and .claude/?

Both are folders inside your project. .agents/skills/ is the standard shared location. .claude/skills/ is optimized for the Claude Code agent.

Can I share skills with my team?

Yes. Skills are plain files in your project folder. Commit them to Git and your entire team gets the same AI instructions.

Why does installation require a workspace?

Skills are tied to a project. Open a folder in your editor first so the extension knows where to place your new skills.

How do I uninstall a marketplace skill?

Go to the LOCAL tab, find the skill, and click the Delete button. You can also disable it temporarily with the toggle switch.
